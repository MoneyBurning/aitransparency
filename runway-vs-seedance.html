Frequently Asked
Is Runway or Seedance cheaper?
Runway Standard at $12/month is cheaper than Dreamina (Seedance) Standard at ~$18/month for international users. Chinese users via Jimeng pay only ~$9.60/month for the identical Seedance model. Importantly, Runway already bundles Seedance 2.0 in its Standard and higher plans — so you can access both for $12/month via Runway alone.
Does Seedance include native audio?
Yes. Seedance 2.0 includes native audio generation, which is a significant advantage over Runway (which exports silent video by default). However, enabling higher resolution and audio simultaneously stacks credit costs up to 6–7× the base rate — a draft clip at $0.60 can become $4+ at full quality with audio.
Can I still access Seedance internationally?
Direct access via Dreamina became invite-only in mid-February 2026 after deepfake videos of celebrities went viral and Disney issued a cease-and-desist. The easiest international access path is now Runway Standard+ (which bundles Seedance 2.0), CapCut's video generation features, or the BytePlus API for developers.
