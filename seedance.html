Frequently Asked
Do Seedance / Dreamina credits roll over?
Monthly subscription credits expire at the end of the billing cycle with no rollover. Only yearly plans (credits valid for 12 months) and one-time credit packs (never expire) carry forward. If your usage is inconsistent month to month, yearly or credit pack purchases prevent waste.
Why is Seedance so hard to access internationally?
ByteDance restricted international Dreamina access in mid-February 2026 after deepfake videos of Tom Cruise and Disney characters generated with Seedance 2.0 went viral. Disney issued a cease-and-desist and the MPA demanded stricter controls. Dreamina's international access shifted to an invite-only Creative Partner Program with no public waitlist. Alternatives include Runway (Seedance 2.0 bundled in Standard+), CapCut, and BytePlus API for developers.
Is Seedance cheaper than Runway or Kling?
Via Jimeng (Chinese-language): yes, significantly cheaper — around $9.60/month. Via Dreamina internationally: comparable to Runway Standard at ~$18/month, but with more credits per dollar on equivalent generations. Via third-party resellers: Atlas Cloud Fast tier starts at ~$0.022/sec, which is the cheapest per-second API rate among all major video AI models including Veo 3.1 and Kling 3.0.
What's new in Seedance 2.5?
Announced June 23, 2026 at Volcano Engine FORCE conference: 30-second native clips (vs 15s in 2.0), 50 multimodal reference inputs (vs fewer in 2.0), native 4K output, 10-bit color depth, 3D white-box preview, and controllable video editing. Public launch expected early July 2026 via Dreamina and Volcano Engine API. Pricing not yet finalized — expect higher credit consumption for 30s/4K generations.
