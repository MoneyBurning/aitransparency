Frequently Asked
Do Midjourney Fast hours roll over each month?
No. All Fast GPU hours expire at the end of every billing cycle with zero rollover — this applies to both monthly and annual subscribers. Annual plans lose proportionally more value if usage is inconsistent.
Is Relax Mode actually unlimited?
Technically yes, but heavy users get deprioritized. At 500+ generations per day, queue times can climb from 2–4 minutes to 10–15 minutes. This soft throttle is not disclosed anywhere in Midjourney's official documentation.
Can I keep client work private on the Standard plan?
No. All Standard plan images are visible in Midjourney's public gallery. You need the Pro plan ($60/mo) for Stealth Mode, which keeps generations private. This is a 100% price increase driven almost entirely by a basic privacy feature.
Is there a free trial for Midjourney in 2026?
No. Midjourney eliminated its free trial program. You must subscribe to a paid plan starting at $10/month to generate even a single image. A very limited trial remains on the Niji Journey mobile app only.
