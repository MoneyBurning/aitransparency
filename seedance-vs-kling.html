Frequently Asked
Is Seedance or Kling AI cheaper?
Kling AI is cheaper at ~$10/month (Standard, annual) vs Seedance's ~$18/month via Dreamina internationally. However, Seedance's identical model is available via Jimeng in China for ~$9.60/month — cheaper than Kling, but restricted to Chinese-language access.
Does Seedance or Kling AI have native audio?
Both do. Kling AI added native audio in version 2.6+ at roughly 2× credit cost. Seedance has included native audio since version 2.0, though stacking higher resolution with audio can multiply credit cost up to 4–7×. Neither discloses the full cost stacking clearly on their pricing pages.
Which is easier to access — Seedance or Kling AI?
Kling AI is open globally with no access restrictions. Seedance's international access via Dreamina became invite-only in mid-February 2026 after a celebrity deepfake scandal prompted a Disney cease-and-desist. Kling wins clearly on access, though it has its own documented issues with credit charges on failed generations and monthly credit expiry.
