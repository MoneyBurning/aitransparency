Frequently Asked
Do Kling AI credits roll over each month?
No. All monthly credits expire at the end of each billing month with zero rollover — even on annual plans. Free daily credits expire after just 24 hours. Inconsistent months on an annual plan result in significant wasted spend with no recourse.
Do failed Kling generations still consume credits?
Yes, based on community reports. Credits are consumed when a generation fails, gets stuck, or produces output that requires a retry. This is not disclosed on the pricing page and significantly inflates the true cost per usable clip — one community estimate puts it at $50+ per minute of polished finished video.
How many videos does the Standard plan actually produce?
Kling markets 660 credits as "33 videos/month" — calculated in Standard Mode (10 credits per 5-second clip). In Professional Mode (1080p, usable quality), the same 660 credits yields 9–18 videos at best, before accounting for failed iterations. Plan around Professional Mode numbers, not the marketing figure.
Is Kling AI free?
There is a free tier with 66 daily credits that reset every 24 hours. Free outputs are watermarked, capped at 720p, and cannot be used commercially. Queue times are longer on the free tier. It is suitable for testing prompts but not for any production workflow.
