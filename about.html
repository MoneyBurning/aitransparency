Why This Exists

AI subscriptions are getting more expensive and more opaque at the same time. Usage limits get cut silently. Credits get replaced with unquantified "compute quotas." Models get swapped without notice. Price stays the same.

We started this database because we were personally frustrated by hitting invisible walls — and discovering after the fact that the limits we thought we had weren't the limits we actually had.

Every report is sourced from official documentation, independent community testing, and investigative coverage. Nothing is vendor-claimed. Everything is dated.

Our Editorial Pledge
✓Every claim is dated and sourced. Official docs, community benchmarks, or investigative coverage — never vendor-provided talking points.
✓No affiliate links. Ever. We do not receive commissions from any of the services we audit. Rankings and scores are not for sale.
✓Estimates are labeled. When we can't independently verify a figure, we mark it ESTIMATED. We never present guesses as facts.
✓All audits stay free. We will never paywall the data. Transparency reporting should be accessible to everyone, not just paid subscribers.
✓We correct errors publicly. Found a mistake? Email us with a source. We'll update the report and note the correction with a date.
Advertising & Revenue Policy

This site is supported by display advertising (Google AdSense) and newsletter sponsorships. Our editorial independence is non-negotiable.

What we will and won't do
✅Display contextual ads that don't influence audit scores or content
✅Accept newsletter sponsorships with full disclosure in every sponsored issue
❌Accept payment to improve a service's transparency score or ranking
❌Include affiliate or referral links to any audited service
❌Remove or soften documented findings at a company's request
