Frequently Asked
Does ChatGPT show how many messages I have left?
No. As of June 2026, OpenAI provides zero usage dashboard for consumer plans. You only learn your limit when you hit it.
When does ChatGPT Plus's limit actually reset?
It's a rolling 3-hour window per message, not a fixed clock. A message sent at 2:00 PM frees its slot at 5:00 PM — not at a round hour. This means burst usage is disproportionately penalizing.
Has ChatGPT Plus ever raised its price?
No. It has stayed at $20/month since February 2023 — the most stable price among major AI subscriptions in this database.
Is GPT-5.5 slow?
Time-to-first-token is around 20–30 seconds on the high-reasoning setting — among the slowest of major assistants for that mode, per Artificial Analysis benchmarks from mid-2026.
Does ChatGPT train on my conversations?
Free-tier conversations are used for training by default. You can opt out via Settings → Data Controls. ChatGPT Plus and Team plans have training off by default.
