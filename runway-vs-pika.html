Frequently Asked
Is Runway or Pika cheaper for professional use?
Runway is actually cheaper for professional/commercial use. Pika's $8/month Standard plan prohibits commercial use — you need Pro at $28/month. Runway's $12/month Standard includes commercial rights. So for any client work, Runway saves $16/month vs Pika Pro.
Which is better for social media content?
Pika wins for fast social media clips. It generates videos faster, has unique branded effects (Pikaffects), and a lower entry price. Runway wins on cinematic quality but its 16-second cap and credit cost per clip make it less suited for high-volume short-form content.
Do failed generations cost credits on both?
Pika explicitly states on its pricing page that credits are deducted whether the generation succeeds or fails. Runway does not explicitly charge per failed generation in the same way, making Runway the safer option for iterative workflows where prompts often need adjustment.
