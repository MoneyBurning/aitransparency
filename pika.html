Frequently Asked
Do failed Pika generations still use credits?
Yes. Pika's official pricing page confirms: "Credits are deducted whether the generation succeeds or fails." This is the top billing complaint across Trustpilot and Reddit. Budget for 3–5 attempts per final clip — your effective video count is significantly lower than the raw credit math suggests.
Can I use Pika videos commercially on the Standard plan?
No. Commercial usage rights are restricted to Pro ($28/month) and Fancy ($76/month) plans. Basic and Standard subscribers cannot use generated content for client work, brand content, or any monetized output. The real professional entry price is $28/month — not the $8/month advertised on most marketing materials.
Is Pika's free tier actually useful?
Only for testing the interface. The free Basic tier provides 80 monthly credits — enough for about 8 short Turbo clips or 1–2 quality 1080p clips. All outputs are watermarked, capped at 480p, and cannot be used commercially. It is a demo, not a production plan.
How many videos does Pika Standard actually produce?
Standard's 700 monthly credits divided by 80 credits per 10-second 1080p clip = roughly 8–9 quality videos per month, after accounting for typical failed iterations. At 720p (40 credits each), you get roughly 17 — but 720p is insufficient for most professional use cases.
