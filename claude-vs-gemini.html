Frequently Asked
Is Claude more transparent than Gemini?
Yes, significantly. Claude scores 52% on this database's transparency scale vs Gemini's 28%. Claude shows usage bars in-app; Gemini's May 2026 quota change removed its only consumer-facing counter.
Which is faster — Claude or Gemini?
Both are fast for standard queries. Gemini 2.5 Flash is slightly faster at ~400–450ms TTFT (estimated). Claude Haiku 4.5 is ~500–650ms TTFT. Both are far faster than GPT-5.5 high-reasoning mode.
Does Gemini have better privacy than Claude?
No. Claude Pro does not train on your data by default — you must opt in. Gemini's conversations are reviewable by default, requiring a manual opt-out via My Activity settings.
Who should pick Gemini over Claude?
Users deeply embedded in Google Workspace — Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Drive. Gemini's core differentiation is that integration. For standalone AI chat, writing, or coding, Claude's transparency and privacy posture are stronger.
