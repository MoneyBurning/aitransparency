Frequently Asked
Do Runway credits roll over each month?
No. All monthly credits expire at the end of every billing cycle with zero rollover — on Standard, Pro, and Max plans alike. Budget for 10–15% credit waste from failed generations and unused remainder.
Is Runway's Unlimited plan actually unlimited?
Not for speed. Explore Mode (the "unlimited" tier) is throttled with 5–20 minute queue times during peak usage. Veo 3 and Veo 3.1 are explicitly excluded from Explore Mode and require paid credits. If you need fast generation, you must use Credits Mode, depleting your 2,250 monthly allocation.
How many videos can I make on the Standard plan?
Runway officially estimates 625 credits = ~25 seconds of Gen-4.5 video. In practice, with 3–5 iteration attempts per final clip, you'll produce roughly 2–5 polished scenes per month — not the dozens the credit count implies.
Does Runway include audio in generated videos?
No. Runway exports silent video by default across all plans. You need a separate audio tool (e.g. ElevenLabs, Adobe Audition) for voiceover or music. This is a notable gap versus Sora and Veo 3.1, which include integrated audio generation.
