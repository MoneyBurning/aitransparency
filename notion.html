Frequently Asked
How many AI responses do I get on Notion Free or Plus?
Exactly 20 — total, not per month. Once exhausted, AI features are locked until you upgrade to Business ($20/user/month). This one-time allocation is not prominently disclosed on the pricing page.
Can I get a refund if I remove a team member mid-cycle?
No. Since September 2024, Notion's billing policy means removing a member mid-cycle generates no refund or credit. You pay for the seat through your next renewal date — which could be up to 11 months away.
Is Notion AI truly unlimited on Business plans?
Notion says "unlimited" but acknowledges an undisclosed fair use policy. Users generating extremely high volumes (reported around 300+ per hour) may be temporarily throttled. The exact threshold is not published.
Do paid plans get higher API rate limits?
No. All Notion plans share the same 3 requests/second API limit. There is no paid tier that unlocks higher API access — overages result in HTTP 429 throttling errors, not charges.
