Frequently Asked
Which is better for privacy, ChatGPT or Claude?
Claude Pro has a stronger default privacy posture — it does not train on your conversations by default, and you must actively opt in. ChatGPT's free tier trains on conversations by default, requiring a manual opt-out in settings.
Does ChatGPT or Claude have a visible usage meter?
Claude shows two progress bars in Settings → Usage (session + weekly). ChatGPT has no usage counter — you discover the limit only when a popup blocks you mid-session.
Is Claude faster than ChatGPT?
Yes, significantly for standard queries. Claude Haiku 4.5 has a reported time-to-first-token in the 500–650ms range. GPT-5.5 in high-reasoning mode is estimated at 20–30s TTFT.
Which has better value at $20/month?
Depends on use case. ChatGPT wins for image/video generation (DALL·E, Sora). Claude wins for response speed, privacy defaults, and transparency. Neither discloses all limits upfront.
