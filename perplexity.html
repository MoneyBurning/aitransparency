Frequently Asked
Did Perplexity cut its research limits?
Yes, significantly. Deep Research dropped from 500+ runs/day in late 2025 to about 20/month in early 2026 — a reduction of over 99% — while the $20/month price stayed the same.
Is regular search still generous?
Yes. Pro Search at 200/week remains healthy. The reduction specifically targeted Deep Research, the multi-step research feature.
Can I make all my searches private?
Only per-session. There is no account-wide global privacy toggle — you need to enable Private mode for each individual session.
Is the free tier worth trying first?
Yes. The free tier includes approximately 5–10 Pro Searches/day, which covers casual research needs without a paid subscription.
