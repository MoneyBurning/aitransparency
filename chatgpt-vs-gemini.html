Frequently Asked
Is ChatGPT or Gemini more transparent about usage limits?
Neither scores well — ChatGPT at 31% and Gemini at 28% are the lowest two in this database. ChatGPT at least publishes a message cap in help docs; Gemini's May 2026 compute system has no published equivalent.
Is Gemini faster than ChatGPT?
Yes, substantially. Gemini 2.5 Flash has an estimated time-to-first-token of 400–450ms in benchmark testing. GPT-5.5 in high-reasoning mode is estimated at 20–30s TTFT.
Did Gemini remove its usage credits?
Yes. On May 20, 2026, Google eliminated the 1,000 monthly AI Credits that came with Google AI Pro, replacing them with a compute-based system with no consumer-facing counter.
Which is better for non-Google users?
ChatGPT — Gemini's core differentiation is Workspace integration. Without Gmail, Docs, and Drive as your primary tools, ChatGPT's broader ecosystem and clearer (if still limited) cap structure offers more predictable value.
