Frequently Asked
Is Runway or Kling AI cheaper?
Kling AI starts cheaper at ~$10/month vs Runway's $12/month. But Kling's effective cost per usable clip is often higher because failed generations still burn credits and Professional Mode costs 3.5× more than Standard Mode. At high volume (50+ videos/month), Runway's Explore Mode becomes more cost-effective since there's no per-video credit cost in that tier.
Which has longer video clips?
Kling AI wins by a wide margin — up to 2 minutes per clip. Runway is limited to 16 seconds maximum, the shortest cap among major AI video tools. This makes Kling far better for narrative content, music videos, or any project requiring sustained scenes without stitching.
Does either include native audio?
Kling AI includes native audio generation in Kling 2.6+ models, though enabling audio doubles the credit cost per second. Runway exports silent video by default across all plans — you need a separate tool like ElevenLabs or Adobe Audition for audio. This is one of Runway's biggest gaps versus competitors.
Which is more transparent?
Runway scores higher at 34% vs Kling's 22%. Kling's Trustpilot rating of 1.3/5 driven by billing complaints, failed generation charges, and cancellation friction makes it the least transparent video AI tool audited by this database. Runway's main gaps are the undisclosed credit expiry and Explore Mode throttling.
