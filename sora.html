Frequently Asked
Is Sora still available in 2026?
The Sora consumer app (web + iOS) was discontinued on April 26, 2026. The Sora 2 API remains live until September 24, 2026, after which all endpoints will be decommissioned. Do not build new integrations on Sora. For video generation, consider Runway Gen-4.5, Kling 3.0, or Google Veo 3.1.
Why did OpenAI shut down Sora?
Primarily unsustainable economics. Cantor Fitzgerald estimated peak inference costs at approximately $15 million per day, against $2.1 million in total lifetime revenue. Additional factors included a 66% drop in app downloads from peak, deepfake and copyright moderation challenges, and the need to redirect GPU resources ahead of OpenAI's planned IPO.
What happened to the Disney deal?
Disney's $1 billion investment and three-year character licensing deal — signed December 2025 to allow Sora to generate content featuring 200+ Disney, Marvel, Pixar, and Star Wars characters — collapsed simultaneously with the April 2026 shutdown. Disney's planned Disney+ integration of Sora-generated content was never launched.
What's the best alternative to Sora?
For cinematic quality: Runway Gen-4.5 ($12–76/mo). For longest clips and motion physics: Kling AI (~$10/mo, up to 2 minutes). For fast social content: Pika ($8/mo). For enterprise with audio: Google Veo 3.1 (Vertex AI, $0.03–$0.40/sec). Each has its own hidden limit traps — see their individual transparency reports on this site.
