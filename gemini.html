Frequently Asked
Did Google remove free Gemini credits?
Yes. On May 20, 2026, Google eliminated the 1,000 free monthly AI Credits that came with Google One AI Pro, replacing them with a compute-based system that varies by prompt complexity.
Is the new Gemini limit system clearer or more confusing?
According to widespread community reports, more confusing — the new system ties limits to prompt complexity rather than a simple, predictable count, and no consumer-facing meter exists for it.
Is Gemini fast?
Yes — Gemini 2.5 Flash has among the lowest time-to-first-token of mainstream assistants tested, estimated in the 400–450ms range in third-party benchmarks.
Is Gemini worth it if I'm not in Google Workspace?
Based on community comparisons, the value proposition weakens significantly outside the Google ecosystem. The core differentiation is Workspace integration — without it, Claude or ChatGPT may offer more consistent utility.
